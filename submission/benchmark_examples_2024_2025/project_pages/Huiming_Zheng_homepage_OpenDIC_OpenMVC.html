    I am now a fourth-year Ph.D. student at School of Electronic and Computer Engineering, Shenzhen Graduate School, Peking University. I am affiliated with National Engineering Laboratory for Video Technology – Shenzhen Division. I focus on learning-based media compression, including image compression, video compression, point cloud compression. If you are interested in my research, please feel free to contact me.
